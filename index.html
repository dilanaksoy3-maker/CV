Profile

Built and scaled Denmark’s first fully online FGU school from concept to 50 active learners nationwide — acting as sole content designer, developer, and teacher. Applies a learner-first approach: identifying needs, removing barriers, and focusing on measurable behaviour change.

Experience designing and delivering digital learning platforms, structured training journeys, and scalable learning materials for diverse learner and employee groups. Works hands-on with the design, development, and daily operation of LMS environments (Moodle), collaborating with leadership and cross-functional teams to translate complex knowledge into clear, practical training programmes.

Designs and delivers structured training across digital modules, video, onboarding flows, and print. Uses continuous feedback loops — learner response, completion data, and direct observation — to iterate, refine, and remove what does not create impact.

Academic background in cultural analysis, SLA/FLA, and cross-cultural communication, combined with applied instructional design experience across varied learner populations and proficiency levels.
